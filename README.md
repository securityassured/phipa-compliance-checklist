# PHIPA Compliance Checklist

A practical, self-assessment checklist for **PHIPA** (Ontario's *Personal Health Information Protection Act, 2004*) — built for the health information custodians who carry the obligation but rarely have a dedicated security team: **dental practices, clinics, physiotherapy and rehab providers, pharmacies, and the vendors that serve them.**

It turns a dense piece of health-privacy legislation into eight clear domains you can work through in an afternoon and re-run every quarter.

## What's inside

**[→ PHIPA-Compliance-Checklist.md](./PHIPA-Compliance-Checklist.md)**

Eight domains, each a set of concrete, markable items:

1. Governance & Accountability
2. Consent & Patient Rights
3. Administrative Safeguards
4. Technical Safeguards
5. Physical Safeguards
6. Breach Management
7. Third-Party & Vendor Risk
8. Retention, Records & Continuous Improvement

## How to use it

1. Open the checklist and work through each domain.
2. Mark every item **✅ In place · 🟨 Partial · ❌ Missing · ➖ N/A**.
3. Prioritize anything that isn't a clean ✅ — especially in Domains 4, 6, and 7, where incidents and regulator scrutiny concentrate.
4. Re-assess quarterly and after any material change to your systems, staff, or vendors.

You can fork this repo and track your own answers privately, or copy the checklist into your internal documentation.
